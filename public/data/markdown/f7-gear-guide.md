# Floor 7 (F7) High-Level Gear Guide
**Author:** lotrszefOP | **Date:** Jan 26, 2026

Welcome to the ultimate guide for Floor 7 mastery. Whether you're an Archer looking to reach the 100% crit chance cap or a Berserker wanting to clear rooms instantly, this guide covers the current meta.

---

## Archer: The Long-Range Powerhouse
Archer remains the dominant class for DPS in F7. Your goal is maximum attack speed and crit chance.

### Armor Progression
- **Mid-Tier:** 3/4 Necron + Maxor's Boots
- **End-Game:** 2/4 Necron + Maxor's Boots + Golden Necron Head

> **BiS Setup:** Golden Necron Head (Diamond) + M7 Skeleton Master Chestplate (or Necron) + Necron Leggings + Maxor's Boots.

**Modifiers:** G5P5Rej5 (Min), Ancient Reforge, 5 Stars, Hot/Fuming Books, Recombed. Gemstones: Sugar Rush 3 recommended. Ultimate: Legion.

### Weapons
| Item | Tier | Modifiers |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Juju Shortbow** | Entry | Rapid/Precise, SE 5, OV 5, Power 6, Quiver 10, Recomb |
| **Terminator** | BiS | Precise (100% CC req), Power 7, Tabasco 3, Toxophilite 10 |

### Stats & Utilities
- **Magical Power:** 600+ for Juju, 900+ for Terminator.
- **Powerstone:** **Itchy** for Juju (67/100 AS), **Fortuitous** for Term.
- **Pets:** Legendary Skeleton (Plushie) or Golden Dragon (Remedies).
